Hospital Supply Conversion Problem

Scenario: St. Mary's Hospital receives medical equipment shipments from both domestic and international suppliers. The shipping department needs to calculate total weights for inventory management.

Solving it for: My Dad(parent)

Sourced link: Wikipedia

Problem: The hospital received the following deliveries today:

3 boxes of surgical instruments, each weighing 15 kilograms
2 boxes of medical devices, each weighing 22 pounds
1 box of laboratory equipment weighing 8.5 kilograms


Questions:

What is the total weight of all deliveries in kilograms?
What is the total weight of all deliveries in pounds?
